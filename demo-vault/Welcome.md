# Welcome to Ax Visual Lab

This is a demo note in your Ax Visual Lab vault. The visual lab can read, search, and organize these notes.

## What you can do

- **Chat** with agents in the Chat & Trace tab
- **Build signatures** in the Builder tab (Ax's type system: `input -> output`)
- **Run code** in the Notebook tab (JavaScript with AxLLM loaded)
- **Evaluate** agents in the Eval & Optimize tab
- **Browse components** in the Components tab — your agents, tools, and flows

## Next steps

1. Try chatting with the demo assistant
2. Create your own agent by editing `crew/registry.yaml`
3. Connect a real Obsidian vault by setting `OBSIDIAN_VAULT_PATH` in `.env`
